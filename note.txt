===================================================
FEATURE: Registration Form & Success Page Overhaul
===================================================

User requests:
  1. Make the add-ons link (certificate / speaker session) more unique
     and visible on the registration page.
  2. Remove the email field from the registration form so people without
     email can register; update the admin view accordingly.
  3. On the registration success page, ask the registrant to join the
     WhatsApp event community and volunteer team.
  4. Also show both WhatsApp links on the registration form page.

---

WHAT WAS CHANGED
-----------------

── 1. Add-ons prompt redesigned (RegisterEvent.jsx) ──────────────────────

BEFORE: A small grey text line + plain underline link at the bottom of
        the form card — easy to miss.

AFTER:  A bold gradient banner card (amber → orange) with:
  • Two decorative semi-transparent circles in the background for depth.
  • An icon badge showing both Award (certificate) and Mic2 (speaker) icons
    side by side inside a frosted white/20 pill.
  • Headline: "Get a Certificate or Book a Speaker Session!"
  • Subtext:  "Limited slots — don't miss out!"
  • A solid white button with orange text at the bottom of the card.
  The contrast between the white button and the orange background makes
  it impossible to miss while still fitting the app's colour scheme.

── 2. Email removed ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Three layers were updated:

  a) Backend — models/Registration.js
     Changed email field from required:true → required:false, default:""
     This means existing records keep their email; new records can omit it.

  b) Backend — controllers/registrationController.js
     - Removed email from the destructured request body.
     - Removed the "!name || !email" validation check (now just "!name").
     - Removed the duplicate-registration check that queried by email,
       because without email there is no reliable unique identifier to
       block duplicates on.
     - Removed email from the Registration.create() call.

  c) Frontend — pages/ViewRegistered.jsx (admin registrants table)
     - Removed the Mail icon import from lucide-react.
     - Removed email from the search/filter function (now searches name only).
     - Updated the search input placeholder to "Search by name...".
     - Removed the Email column header from both the desktop grid
       (7-col → 6-col: grid-cols-[1fr_1fr_1fr_1fr_90px_80px]) and the
       mobile grid (5-col → 4-col: grid-cols-[1fr_1fr_72px_60px]).
     - Removed the email <span> cells from both the desktop and mobile
       row layouts.

  d) Frontend — pages/RegisterEvent.jsx (registration form)
     - Removed email: "" from the form state object.
     - Deleted the entire email validation block (regex check).
     - Deleted the Email input field from the JSX form.

── 3. WhatsApp links on the registration form (RegisterEvent.jsx) ────────

Added two clickable rows below the add-ons banner, inside the form card:

  Row 1 — Event Community
    • Green circle avatar with "W", colour #25D366 (WhatsApp brand green).
    • Label: "Join Event Community" / subtext: "Get updates on WhatsApp".
    • Clicking opens: https://chat.whatsapp.com/BNgu49pWypiCutHDe?s=cl&p=a&mlu=4
    • Opens in a new tab (target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer").

  Row 2 — Volunteer Team
    • Teal circle avatar with "V", colour #128C7E (WhatsApp dark teal).
    • Label: "Join Volunteer Team" / subtext: "Be part of the team!".
    • Clicking opens: https://chat.whatsapp.com/j9o02sRit1gLCe26Lv8Y1F

  Both rows use bg-green-50 with a green border, and include a small
  "Join →" label on the right that shifts slightly on hover.

── 4. WhatsApp cards on the registration SUCCESS page (RegisterEvent.jsx) ─

After the certificate and speaker booking prompt cards, two new styled
cards were inserted before the "Back to Events" button:

  Card 1 — WhatsApp Community (green theme)
    • bg-green-50, border-2 border-green-200.
    • "W" badge in #25D366 background.
    • Heading: "Join Our WhatsApp Community!"
    • Description: "Stay updated with event info, reminders, and
      announcements."
    • Full-width button in #25D366 green: "Yes, Join Community →"
    • Same community link as above.

  Card 2 — Volunteer Team (teal theme)
    • bg-teal-50, border-2 border-teal-200.
    • "V" badge in #128C7E background.
    • Heading: "Do You Want to Join Our Volunteer Team?"
    • Description explains this is for the event team's WhatsApp group.
    • Full-width button in #128C7E teal: "Yes, Join Volunteer Group →"
    • Same volunteer link as above.

  The speaker card's bottom margin was adjusted from mb-6 → mb-4 to
  keep consistent spacing with the two new cards added after it.
